HUONG DAN DEMO WEB THAI VU - BAN TOI UU
======================================

Muc tieu:
- Demo nhanh, ro, khong loi nhap nhang giua Client va Admin.
- Tao cam giac "web da san sang van hanh that".

--------------------------------------------------
1) CHUAN BI TRUOC KHI DEMO (5-10 PHUT)
--------------------------------------------------

1.1 Khoi dong he thong
- Mo 2 terminal:
  Terminal 1: chay backend
  Terminal 2: chay frontend
- Kiem tra nhanh:
  + Backend API con song
  + Frontend mo duoc trang chu

1.2 Du lieu mau
- Dam bao da co:
  + San pham (du anh, gia, bien the)
  + Danh muc (de demo menu danh muc)
  + Banner (co it nhat 2-3 banner)
  + Bai viet gioi thieu + huong dan
- Tao san 2 tai khoan:
  + Admin
  + Khach hang thuong

1.3 Moi truong demo
- Dong bot tab khong lien quan.
- Tat thong bao he thong (neu co).
- Zoom trinh duyet 100%.
- Dung mang on dinh.

--------------------------------------------------
2) KICH BAN DEMO DE XUAT (15-20 PHUT)
--------------------------------------------------

[PHAN A - CLIENT: 7-9 PHUT]

A1. Trang chu + banner
- Vao trang chu.
- Noi nhanh:
  "Trang chu co banner slider tu dong, co nut dieu huong, co cham phan trang."
- Chuyen slide bang nut trai/phai.

A2. Danh muc va bo loc
- Mo menu Danh muc san pham.
- Chon 1 danh muc -> trang /shop?category=...
- Cho thay loc dung theo danh muc.
- Su dung bo loc nang cao (gia, hang, dong xe) de thu hep ket qua.

A3. Chi tiet san pham + gio hang
- Mo 1 san pham.
- Chon bien the, so luong.
- Bam "Them vao gio hang" (cho thay hieu ung phan hoi).
- Vao gio hang, cap nhat so luong.

A4. Don mua
- Dang nhap tai khoan khach.
- Vao profile -> Don mua.
- Xem trang thai don va chi tiet don.

[PHAN B - NOI DUNG: 3-4 PHUT]

B1. Trang Gioi thieu
- Bam nut "Gioi thieu" tren header.
- Noi:
  "Trang nay lay bai gioi thieu moi nhat tu he thong noi dung."

B2. Trang Huong dan
- Bam "Huong dan".
- Chon bai ben trai, noi dung doi ben phai theo bai duoc chon.

[PHAN C - ADMIN: 6-8 PHUT]

C1. Quan ly banner
- Dang nhap admin -> vao Banner.
- Them 1 dong text cho banner, doi thu tu, bat/tat hien thi.
- Luu xong quay lai trang chu de cho thay thay doi cap nhat.

C2. Quan ly noi dung
- Vao "Noi dung".
- Sua nhanh tieu de/1 doan ngan cua bai gioi thieu.
- Luu va mo lai trang Gioi thieu de doi chieu ngay.

C3. Quan ly van hanh
- Vao Don hang / San pham / Ton kho / Khach hang.
- Nhan manh:
  "Tat ca da co phan trang 10 muc/trang de de quan ly."

--------------------------------------------------
3) CAU NOI GOI Y KHI DEMO
--------------------------------------------------

- "Web nay tach ro 2 vai tro: khach mua hang va quan tri vien."
- "Moi noi dung quan trong o trang chu co the doi ngay tu trang admin."
- "He thong huong den thao tac nhanh tai cua hang: bat/tat, sua nhanh, cap nhat truc tiep."
- "Giao dien da toi uu cho mobile va desktop."

--------------------------------------------------
4) NHUNG DIEM NEN NHAN MANH (USP)
--------------------------------------------------

- Banner dong + text layer linh hoat (khong can sua code).
- Danh muc/loc san pham ro rang, tim san pham nhanh.
- Luong mua hang day du: gio hang -> dat hang -> theo doi don.
- Khu admin de dung, co phan trang, phu hop van hanh hang ngay.
- Noi dung Gioi thieu/Huong dan cap nhat truc tiep.

--------------------------------------------------
5) CHECKLIST TRANH SU CO KHI DEMO
--------------------------------------------------

- [ ] Frontend dang chay

Neu co loi:

6) THU TU DEMO CUC NHANH (3 PHUT)
--------------------------------------------------

1) Trang chu (banner + danh muc)
2) San pham -> Them vao gio
3) Admin -> Sua banner 1 click
4) Quay lai trang chu xac nhan thay doi
5) Mo Gioi thieu/Huong dan
